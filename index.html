Friend of Society

Lighting the Path to Education.

At Sanghamitra Foundation, we believe in the power of community. Through our local teacher networks, we provide dedicated after-school study hours for rural children, ensuring they thrive in their educational journey.

Gather

Our teachers identify children who need extra help and gather them in safe, comfortable local environments after school hours.

Teach

Through guided revision and interactive study sessions, we help students master their subjects and build confidence.

Nurture

Beyond academics, we foster a sense of community and social responsibility, true to our name Sanghamitra.
